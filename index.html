About Me

I am a Master's student in the Department of Mathematics and Statistics (concentration: Mathematics – thesis) at the University of Ottawa. I am very fortunate to be advised by Dr. Andrej Bogdanov, Dr. Arthur Mehta, and Dr. Monica Nevins. My research interests lie broadly in the algebraic and geometric formulation of topics in theoretical physics and theoretical computer science.

Prior to graduate school, I obtained a Bachelor of Science through the Enrichment Stream in Theoretical Physics at the Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), where Dr. Pak Ming Hui was my undergraduate advisor.

Areas of Interest

  • Representation theory and algebraic combinatorics
  • Convex real algebraic geometry and semidefinite optimization
  • Noncommutative geometry, non-local games, and quantum complexity
  • Algebraic complexity and GCT
